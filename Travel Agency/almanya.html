Meininger Hotel München City Center

Located in Munich, within 1.9 km of Olympiapark and 2.8 km of BMW Welt, MEININGER Hotel München Olympiapark provides accommodation with parking and free WiFi. The private bathrooms include a shower, free toiletries and a hair dryer. A continental breakfast buffet and packed lunch are available each morning at the property.
