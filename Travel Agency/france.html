Cheap Hotels

Motel One Paris-Porte Dorée

Motel One Paris-Porte Dorée is a modern and affordable hotel in the 12th arrondissement of Paris. It offers stylish rooms with essential amenities and a cozy lobby bar. Conveniently located near public transport, guests can easily access attractions like the Palais de la Porte Dorée and Bois de Vincennes. This motel provides a comfortable and welcoming experience for travelers in the City of Light.

⭐⭐

Chatillon Paris Montparnasse

Châtillon Paris Montparnasse is a charming hotel located in the Montparnasse district of Paris. Known for its warm atmosphere and comfortable accommodations, the hotel offers a range of well-furnished rooms that cater to both leisure and business travelers. Guests appreciate the convenient access to public transportation, making it easy to explore the city’s iconic landmarks. With nearby cafes and shops, Châtillon Paris Montparnasse provides a pleasant base for discovering the vibrant culture and history of Paris.

Kyriad Hotel XIII Italie Gobelins

The Kyriad Hotel XIII Italie Gobelins is a cozy hotel in Paris's 13th arrondissement, near the Gobelins area. It offers comfortable rooms and is close to attractions like the Jardin des Plantes. With good public transport access, it's an ideal spot for exploring the city, making it perfect for both leisure and business travelers.
